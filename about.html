Reason of existence

Nasa and SpaceX are companies working on sending humans to Mars and thus turn humanity into multiplanetary species. Almendra is working on solving potential human difficulties in their process and thus collaborate in the colonization of Mars.

Serving as an open research community and mixing up two exciting areas: space and artificial intelligence, we are enacting useful resources for the next major triumph of humanity, leaving the seat of a spectator for the paper of an actor.
