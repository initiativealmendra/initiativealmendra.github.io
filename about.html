Reason of existence

With climate change, population growth, epidemics, overdue asteroid strikes, our planet is increasingly precarious, and there is no backup plan to preserve the continuity of humankind; Because although billionaire entrepreneurs are trying to create rockets fit for human travel and government agencies spend billions furthering their explorations, we are still a long way off from making our way to colonize another planet. It is for this reason that Almendra exists.

Serving as a worldwide open AI-research initiative for space exploration, Almendra seeks to bring people around the world to join efforts with space agencies and, through massive collaboration, close technological gaps that allow faster progress in making humans a truly spacefaring race.
